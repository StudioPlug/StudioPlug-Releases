## What StudioPlug Does

StudioPlug helps music creators move faster from inspiration to creation.

It lets producers, rappers, singers, and songwriters capture musical ideas from supported beat pages and turn them into usable studio material in seconds. With the StudioPlug YouTube extension and desktop app, users can detect useful information from a beat, organize their creative workflow, and prepare everything they need before recording.

StudioPlug is designed to simplify the early creative process:

- Paste or detect a supported beat link
- Prepare the audio for your local workflow
- Analyze the BPM and musical key
- Identify useful beat information
- Save downloads and analysis in a built-in library with an integrated audio player
- Move faster into your DAW workflow

The goal is simple: remove friction between finding inspiration and making music.

Instead of wasting time switching between websites, converters, BPM tools, key detectors, folders, and notes, StudioPlug brings the workflow into one clean creator-focused experience.

StudioPlug is built for modern music creators who want a faster, cleaner, and more professional way to start a session.

StudioPlug is designed for content you own, created, licensed, or are authorized to use.

---

## Core Features

- **BPM detection** with half-time and double-time context
- **Key detection** with Camelot and Auto-Tune-ready musical information
- **BeatStars link detection** when a valid link is available
- **Local library** with covers, history, favorites, and playback
- **DAW-ready export workflow** for faster session setup
- **YouTube companion** to send supported page context into the desktop app
- **Local-first desktop workflow** for files, analysis, and history
